Description:

Calculates the amount of storage space available for Content and Flowfile Repositories, gathers system and JVM statuses, and processor queue sizes, and emits these metrics to Amazon CloudWatch.

Current Limitations:

Due to packaging issues in NiFi, there isn't a sane way to import AWSCredentialsProviderService. Including the AWSCredentialsProviderService class in this service redefines the service in NiFi, and makes a second copy of the service appear in the service creation list. Due to this issue, this service can only use a DefaultAWSCredentialsProviderChain to connect to CloudWatch.
